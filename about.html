About Prime Power Devices

Founded in 2001, Prime Power Devices is dedicated to manufacturing high-performance TO-3, TO-3P, and TO-220 package power transistors. For over 22 years, the company has served as a trusted subcontractor to Bharat Electronics Ltd., earning recognition for its quality and reliability in defense and communication applications. Its customers include leading OEMs in telecommunications, broadcasting, public address systems, industrial electronics, and defense sectors. Today, Prime Power Devices proudly markets its own brand of power devices, continuing a tradition of precision engineering, stringent quality standards, and dependable customer support — making it a trusted partner where performance and reliability are critical.

About Narotham Reddy

With over 40 years of experience in the semiconductor industry, Narotham Reddy has built a career rooted in innovation, reliability, and quality. He began his journey in 1980 with SSDIL (later Greaves Semiconductor) and went on to work with ETTDC, Keltron, and Teamasia Lakhi Semiconductor. In 2001, he founded Prime Power Devices, focusing on high-quality TO-3 packaged power transistors for Bharat Electronics Ltd. and other leading clients. Under his leadership, the company has grown into TO-3P and TO-220 device manufacturing, expanding its reach in both Indian and global markets. An Electronics and Communications Engineering graduate from Osmania University, Hyderabad, Narotham Reddy continues to lead Prime Power Devices with a commitment to excellence and long-term partnerships.
