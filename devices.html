TO-3 Transistor Package

The TO-3 transistor package is a metal can type casing widely used for power semiconductors due to its durability, reliability, and excellent thermal performance. Known for its robust design, it provides superior heat dissipation and can handle high currents and voltages, making it ideal for demanding applications such as power amplifiers, voltage regulators, and industrial control systems. With its secure mounting and efficient heat transfer, the TO-3 package remains a trusted choice for engineers seeking long-lasting performance in high-power electronic circuits.

MJ15024

MJ15025

MJ2955

TO-3P Transistor Package

The TO-3P transistor package is a high-power plastic casing designed to deliver excellent thermal efficiency and mechanical stability in compact form. Unlike the metal TO-3, the TO-3P offers a lighter, cost-effective solution while maintaining the ability to handle high current and voltage levels. Its flat design with a large heat sink mounting surface ensures efficient heat dissipation, making it well-suited for power transistors, MOSFETs, and IGBTs in applications such as audio amplifiers, switching power supplies, and motor control systems. Trusted for performance and reliability, the TO-3P package is a popular choice in both industrial and consumer electronics.

TO-220 Transistor Package

The TO-220 package is a versatile and widely used casing for power semiconductors, offering a perfect balance between compact size and high-performance capability. With its three-pin design and exposed metal tab, it provides efficient heat dissipation, making it ideal for mounting on heatsinks in high-current and high-voltage applications. Commonly used for transistors, MOSFETs, voltage regulators, and power diodes, the TO-220 package ensures reliable operation in audio amplifiers, switching power supplies, and motor control circuits. Its durability, ease of handling, and thermal efficiency make it a standard choice for both industrial and consumer electronics.
